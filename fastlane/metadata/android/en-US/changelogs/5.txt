🚀 v2.1.0

We’re continuing to improve the app to make managing your fermentation processes easier and clearer:

📅 New fermentation calendar: organise and view both your current and future processes at a glance.
🎨 Visual improvements to cards: it’s now easier to tell the difference between kefir and kombucha fermentations.
🛠️ Internal code update: components have been adapted to the latest Flutter recommendations for better performance and maintenance.
📝 Detailed view of fermentations: access each card from the main screen or history and add notes during or after the process.

Thank you for continuing to ferment with us! 🫙
